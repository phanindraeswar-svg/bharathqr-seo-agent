# Search Engine Submission Checklist

Use this after the first successful production deployment.

## First 30 minutes

1. Open `https://www.bharathqr.com/robots.txt`.
2. Open `https://www.bharathqr.com/sitemap.xml`.
3. Confirm `/tools`, `/solutions`, `/use-cases`, `/trust`, `/materials`, `/comparisons`, `/templates`, and `/cases` appear in the sitemap.
4. Submit sitemap in Google Search Console.
5. Submit sitemap in Bing Webmaster Tools.
6. Use URL Inspection for the highest-priority URLs in `INDEXING_WATCHLIST.md`.

## Priority submit order

1. Homepage
2. Tools directory
3. UPI QR generator
4. Google Review QR generator
5. URL QR generator
6. Text QR generator
7. WhatsApp QR generator
8. Restaurants solution
9. Kirana stores solution
10. UPI QR safety page

## Do not panic if

- Most pages are discovered but not indexed immediately.
- Google indexes only a small subset in the first few days.
- Search Console data is delayed by 24–72 hours.

Focus on crawlability, internal links, and stable deployment first.
