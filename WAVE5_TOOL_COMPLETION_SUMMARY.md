# Wave 5 Tool Completion Summary

This wave adds five lightweight, client-side QR tools that are useful for Indian businesses and print-first QR use cases.

## Added Tools

- Event QR Generator
- Email QR Generator
- SMS QR Generator
- Phone Call QR Generator
- PDF QR Generator from PDF/document URL

## Why these were added

These tools expand BharathQR beyond payments and reviews into everyday business communication: events, enquiries, calls, SMS, brochures, menus and PDF documents. They are static, fast, mobile-first, and require no login or backend.

## Still delayed

- Full Logo QR builder with upload and center-logo embedding
- Dynamic QR backend
- File hosting/upload backend
- AI text generation APIs
- User accounts or dashboards
