Previous waves added pages and clusters. This wave makes the system safer and more launch-ready by checking quality, route coverage and internal-link consistency before deployment.

## Content rule now locked

Every article should naturally combine:

- Tool keyword
- Business or industry keyword
- App keyword where relevant
- Pain point
- Benefit
- Action word
- CTA to BharathQR

No keyword stuffing. No generic AI filler. No content unless it supports a tool, solution, use case, material, trust page, comparison, template or AI business foundation.
