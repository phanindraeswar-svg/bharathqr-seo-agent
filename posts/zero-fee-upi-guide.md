### Why Payment Gateway Fees Are Eating Your Profits
Every time a customer swipes a card or uses a third-party app via a standard gateway, you lose 1% to 2% of that sale to processing fees. For a growing retail store or kirana shop, that translates to thousands of rupees lost every single month.

### The BharathQR Advantage
By routing customer payments directly through the decentralized UPI network using an authenticated BharathQR asset, your business bypasses middleman processing layers entirely.

* **Instant Settlement:** Funds land directly in your linked current account.
* **0% MDR:** Absolutely zero merchant discount rate or hidden maintenance percentages.
* **Universal App Support:** Customers can scan using BHIM, Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm interchangeably.
